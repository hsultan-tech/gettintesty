==Release notes_Scotia==

Compiler of release notes: Dalton Maag
Designer: Dalton Maag
Designer URL: daltonmaag.com

Date of creation: 2019-05-31

Changes since previous release:
- Extended character set to All European Latin.
- Added Italic and Legal styles.

Basic information
1 Basic Family name: Scotia
2 TrueType Version record: 1.100
3 Regional package: Latin
4 Supported scripts: Latin
5 Number of glyphs: 509
6 Weights/Styles: Light, Light Italic, Regular, Italic, Bold, Bold Italic, Headline, Legal
7 Usage: Text and Display

Technology
1 The files have been delivered in the TrueType-based OpenType format, for use in all major Operating Systems, and also as TTF for use in apps, and in web font format: WOFF; WOFF2.
2 Embedding settings: Everything is allowed (installable mode); subsetting is allowed.
3 Hinting information: All glyphs are manually hinted for optimized rasterization in Microsoft ClearType or for other LCD devices.
4 Default numerals: Proportional. Tabular figures are accessible through OpenType Features.

Other Information
The following logos are accessible through the Contextual Alternates (calt) feature, by typing the corresponding sequence (case-sensitive):
    To access the Scotiabank Logo: ScotiabankLogo
    To access the Scotia Logo: ScotiaLogo
    To access the Flying S: FlyingSLogo
    To access the Banque Scotia Logo: BanqueScotiaLogo
    To access the Corporate Logo: CorporateLogo
